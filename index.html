Biography

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), working with Prof. Corey W. Arnold.. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Washington with Prof. Chengcheng Zhu. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Rui Zheng. I was also a Visiting Student at the Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC), A*STAR, Singapore, mentored by Dr. Huazhu Fu.

My research focuses on AI for personalized and accessible healthcare:

  • Personalized Cancer Care: improving disease monitoring from longitudinal data through multimodal learning, temporal modeling and data synthesis.
  • Accessible Ultrasound Imaging : enabling non-expert operators to acquire and interpret ultrasound through AI-guided scanning and automated diagnosis.
