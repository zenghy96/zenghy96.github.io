Hongye Zeng 曾宏业

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU) in 2017. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, and intelligent medical image analysis, with a primary focus on ultrasound images and ophthalmic images.
